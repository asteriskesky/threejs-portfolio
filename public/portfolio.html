PROCESS

How I work

Six principles that guide my approach to building products.

01

User problems first

I start with the problem, not the solution. Every feature should trace back to a real user need.

02

Ship to learn

Perfect is the enemy of shipped. I believe in getting things in front of users fast and iterating.

03

Write it down

Clear documentation creates alignment. PRDs, specs, and decision logs reduce ambiguity.

04

Sweat the details

The difference between good and great products is in the micro-interactions and edge cases.

05

Understand the stack

I don't hand off specs I can't explain technically. Understanding constraints leads to better solutions.

06

Over-communicate

Stakeholder alignment isn't a one-time event. I keep everyone informed and involved.
